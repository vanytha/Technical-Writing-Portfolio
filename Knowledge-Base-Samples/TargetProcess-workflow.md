## Client Request to Production Deployment Workflow 

This workflow outlines the end-to-end process followed for handling client development requests in the Targetprocess system. 
> Targetprocess is an agile project management platform used to plan, track, and manage software development workflows.

It begins with ticket intake and triage, followed by Business Analysis and Change Request Assessment (CRA) creation, where requirements are clarified and development and QA estimates are obtained.

Once the CRA is approved by the client, the request moves into the development lifecycle, including feature creation, prioritization, iteration planning, development, and quality assurance. The process also includes review demos, user acceptance testing (UAT), and validation by the client.

After successful testing, the feature is deployed to production. The workflow concludes with documentation completion, invoicing, and formal closure of the request.
